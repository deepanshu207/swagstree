How images work

Main images — For home screen, size charts, and lifestyle shots. Also used as a fallback when a variant has no photo. Use “show at Start/End” to append these before or after variant photos in the detail gallery.

Variant gallery images — Photos tied to a specific size/color/pattern. Customers see these when they select that variant.

360° product spin — Separate rotation photos (Amazon/Flipkart style). Customers swipe to rotate the product — not a Google Street View panorama.
